What is a
Placidity Pool?

Placidity Pools don’t rely on the harsh chemicals used to sanitise conventional swimming pools. Our technology represents Australia’s most advanced sustainable natural pool technology, creating a fresh water feeling in your completely chemical- free swimming pool.

Micro-organisms steadily eliminate contaminants from water and keep the biological balance in nature. Placidity pools replicate this system to maintain a healthy water balance using a Bio-Tank, a shallow-planted biological filter zone.

Our Mission

Placidity’s mission is to revolutionise swimming into a natural experience that won’t rely on the harsh chemicals used to sanitise conventional swimming pools.

Placidity…Swim in Nature combines 60 years of expertise in pool design and construction with tropical inspiration to invent our specialised water purification system, creating a unique natural pool that is designed as a reflection of you that will grow stronger overtime.

The Placidity
Difference

Placidity’s ingenuity , technology and the EcoSplash™ patented system of natural biofilm cultivation use a layered arrangement of natural media, specific water flow rates and aquatic plants to produce crystal clean, pure water.

Placidity use Micro-organisms to steadily eliminate contaminants from the water and keep the biological balance as found in natural water. Placidity pools replicate nature to maintain a healthy water balance using a Bio-Tank, a shallow-planted biological filter zone.

Conventional swimming pools use lots of chemical additives. This is not necessary in a natural pool. The organic self-cleaning process, together with regular maintenance, provides you with trouble free swimming pleasure.

The pleasure of swimming in natural water is hard to describe and needs to be experienced.

NO CHEMICALS

Sensitive skin, sore and itchy red eyes, nasty fumes and bleached bathers – the effects of chemicals and additives in swimming pools are well known. You are breathing and swimming in a concoction of toxins. A Placidity Pool eliminates those problems. Take care of you and your family’s health and wellbeing with one of our pools that is naturally filtered.

ECOLOGICALLY SAFE

Chemical pools are having a negative effect on our environment. Urban salinity (too much salt) is a ticking time bomb. Chemical run off into our precious waterways harms animals and marine life. Placidity Pools’ natural pools will not contribute to that ecological crisis. We are taking care of the environment – one pool at a time – by enabling you to keep your pool water clean and fresh by natural means. It’s the only responsible pool option for everyone’s future.

REDUCED RUNNING COSTS

Ever heard the saying: “Pools are a hole in the ground that you keep throwing money into”? That’s because of the "running" cost of equipment (and chemicals needed to run a traditional pool. Placidity Pools harnesses nature combined with cutting edge technology to ensure significant energy efficiencies and savings. Your power bill is cut drastically and the pool chemical bill is eliminated.

NATURAL SURROUNDS

Placidity Pools are so much more than a place to swim – our goal is to reconnect you to nature, in your own backyard. Increased biodiversity through living plants and organisms, that naturally filter the water, is not only beneficial for the environment but also your body, mind and spirit. Our Technology
